Doctrine — locked alongside the CC mockups

SP is the simplified twin. Reps see the next-up lead and their own counter while dialing — plus, now, the live team ribbon so peer pressure shows up in the moment. Director gets a 5-row quick-glance with deep-link buttons into CC.

CC /outreach is the master record. Audio playback, post-day fill-in, "Send as [rep]" impersonation, reassign, lead timeline drill-down — all CC-only. SP's job is volume and rhythm; CC's job is depth and review.

The missing piece SP needed was the live ribbon on the dialer. The standalone Leaderboard already exists but reps had to navigate away from the dialer to see it. The ribbon makes peer stats visible during the dial — that's the unlock.

↗ purple in CC = SP-sourced. Here in SP, ↗ purple = CC-sourced (lead roster, callback notes, recommended-next).
